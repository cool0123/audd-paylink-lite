# AUDD PayLink Lite Sponsor Update

## Short update

Hi AUDD team,

I created a public MVP demo for `AUDD PayLink Lite`, a lightweight merchant payment-link and settlement dashboard concept for AUDD on Solana.

## What is included

- Merchant payment-link creation flow.
- AUDD-denominated checkout preview.
- QR-ready demo payment page.
- Payment dashboard with pending / paid / expired states.
- CSV settlement export for basic reconciliation.
- Clear safety boundary: no private keys, no wallet signing, no live payment execution in the demo.

## Why it fits AUDD

The product is designed around a practical Australian-dollar stablecoin use case: small merchants and creators need a simple way to price invoices in AUDD, share a checkout link, receive AUDD on Solana, and reconcile settlement references.
